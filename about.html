About Hurricane Design Build Group

Hurricane Design-Build Group is a diversely talented collection of building designers, engineers and contractors who work together in a team environment applying specialized service to a variety of construction project types and sizes throughout the state of Florida.

Our professionals represent over 125 years of experience in the building industry. Our team is comprised of professional engineers, building designers and general contractors who have experience ranging from building design, surveys, and construction document development, to engineering, permitting, project management, and construction contracting.

The practice of inventive, purposeful, and cost effective design, engineering and construction services is our focus. We offer these services in part or in whole to serve our clients, and we are happy to collaborate with other professionals. Our Mission is Supporting Your Mission by acting as a resolute partner in the success of your building project.
