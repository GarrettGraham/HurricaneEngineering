Structural, Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing.

Need certified construction documents for permit? Developing a new green project and need energy calculations? Have a unique structure in need of unconventional load solutions? Need HVAC computations for your new venue? Want to put up a retaining wall to stabilize your land?

Our Professional Engineers represent over 53 years of friendly solution based experience, from theme park rides and town home development, to interior build outs, custom homes and small additions.

Engineering elements including structural integrity, material cost, sustainability and efficiency are considered when formulating certifiable solutions on a project specific basis.

Design
Let Us Show You How

Whether you call it integrated, whole or complete structure design, the practice of designing buildings that simultaneously take design, structural engineering, and material costs into account has always been a part of our process. We take this concept further by integrating aesthetics, energy efficiency, and sustainability.

The idea that an affordable building has to be unattractive, lack safety or sustainability is old school. It is possible to design affordable homes and buildings while simultaneously applying the principles of artistic, environmentally responsible, and safe design. Let us show you how. Minimize cost, maximize style, minimize energy usage, maximize safety, minimize waste, and maximize space…

…these are just a few of the design elements we can integrate into your building. When your design is complete, you have the option of using our Engineers to complete and certify your construction documents for permit or have your own professionals take over the project. We enjoy building collaborative partnerships with others in the industry.
